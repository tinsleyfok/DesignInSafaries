    Assignment: Zoo Visit + Ethogram

    In this week, the class went to Central Park Zoo and we were asked to observe an animal for at least 30mins. From the Polar Circle zone, I chose one of the penguins to observe since I did lots of research on polar bear previously. I would be better to keep observing the similar species.


    Penguin | 3/2/2019 12:50PM - 1:20PM

    I used pen and paper to record during observation. I divided the behavior into pool behaviors and out of pool behaviors. Pool behaviors including surface swimming, underwater swimming and porpoising. Out of pool behaviors including inactive, locomotion, preening, affiliative and feeding.
